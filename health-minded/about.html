About Us

Heartminded Therapy is commited to provide quality Mental Health Service to individuals and families in need in IIUM and Gombak area. Heartminded Therapy believes that

mental health care is a right, not a privilege. Anyone who needs counseling should have it. We believe the best of people. Professionals with clinical experience and license

in Malaysia are always here to help you !

Salihah Zayn

Salihah received her Master of Clinical Psychology from Universiti
